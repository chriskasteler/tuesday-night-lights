League Information

Season Overview

5-Week Regular Season + 2-Week Playoffs

6 Teams • 6 Players per Team • Each team fields 4 players per week

Schedule

Every Tuesday

5:30pm or 5:00pm Shotgun Start

9-Hole Matches

Scoring

Match Play Format

Win = 2 points

Split = 1 point

Loss = 0 points

Modified net scoring TBD

Team Structure

6 players per team

Each team will field 2 two-man teams each week. Two players will sit.

Playoffs

Top 4 teams advance

Semifinals & Finals

Plus 3rd place match

League Rules

  • Modified Net Format TBD
  • Each player must play at least 3 of the 5 regular season matches.
  • No player can play all 5 regular season matches.
  • All players must play at least once in Playoffs.

What Makes This League Special

• Different format each week keeps everyone engaged
• High-Low and Combined Score formats ensure both players contribute
• Round-robin regular season - everyone plays everyone
• Players vote on playoff formats
• Payouts for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place
